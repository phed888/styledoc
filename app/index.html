GCO UX Principles

  • Reliable and trustworthy experiences

    Our users are confident that the information and processes they encounter are consistent, accurate and genuine, and that there are no surprises.

  • Interactions are intuitive and efficient

    Our users can effortlessly complete their tasks and resolve issues. We don't expose our corporate and organizational seams.

  • We treat our users like humans

    Our users deserve dynamic interactions relative to real people. Design elements are friendly, empathetic and approachable, and always include an authentic voice.

  • Modern and simplified designs matter

    We shield our users from complex workflows and policies with easy responsive designs. Create clean, consistent interactions that effortlessly travel across channels and platforms.

  • We design for our users and not ourselves

    Understanding our users from all angles so we can provide contextual, just-in-time actions that anticipate needs from end to end.
